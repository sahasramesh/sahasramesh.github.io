Computer programming is a highly-applicable skill that can be applied to dozens of professions, from software and mechanical engineering to healthcare and economics.

CamelCase’s mission is to make coding accessible to people of all ages, foster programming communities at the local level, and prepare today’s youth for a computer-centric future.
